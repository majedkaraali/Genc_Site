About Us

Overview

Welcome to Tarsus University Young Informaticians Student Community, an ambitious and forward-thinking team of young professionals equipped with dynamic and 21st-century skills. Our community strives for excellence and aims to reach the summit of the technology, information, and internet industry.

Industry

We operate in the technology, information, and internet industry, where innovation and cutting-edge solutions drive progress and shape the future.

Company Size

With a dedicated team of 11-50 employees, we work together to leverage our collective skills and expertise to achieve remarkable results. We believe in collaboration and foster an environment where everyone's ideas and contributions are valued.

LinkedIn Presence

Our team consists of 18 talented individuals who are passionate about their work. You can find our members on LinkedIn, where they showcase their skills and expertise. Many of them are affiliated with the Young Informaticians Student Community, including both full-time and part-time roles.

Headquarters

Located in Mersin, Tarsus, our community benefits from a vibrant and growing tech scene. We draw inspiration from our surroundings and actively contribute to the development of the local technology ecosystem.

Founded

Tarsus University Young Informaticians Student Community was founded in 2022 with a clear vision of empowering young individuals and fostering their growth in the field of informatics. Since our inception, we have been committed to providing valuable opportunities, resources, and support to aspiring informaticians.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower young individuals and foster their growth in the field of informatics. We believe that by providing valuable opportunities, resources, and support, we can help aspiring informaticians reach their full potential and make a positive impact on the world.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading community for young informaticians in Turkey. We want to be a place where people can come together to learn, collaborate, and innovate. We believe that by working together, we can make a positive impact on the world.
